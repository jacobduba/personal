OSS Contributions

I have contributed to several open source projects in the past year. I added a new keyboard shortcut to the GNOME Terminal emulator to zoom in and out of the terminal window using the Ctrl + Scroll wheel keys. I improved the performance of the AWS Lambda Builders by preventing them from cloning the TypeScript source code to a scratch directory before compiling it. I fixed a regression in the Miniflux RSS reader that caused it to reset the touch-item state of unread articles even if the user was not viewing the unread articles list. I also prevented the Miniflux RSS reader from resetting the touch-item state of articles that have been successfully swiped away.

Web forum software

SKFS is a web 2.0 application where users can sign up and sign in to create, like, and comment on posts. It is designed with a robust client-server REST API, using Express.js and SQLite on the back end and React on the front end. SKFS allows users to connect with each other and share their thoughts and ideas through posts. Users can also like and comment on posts to interact with other users and share their thoughts. SKFS is a powerful and versatile platform that can be used for a variety of purposes, such as social networking, education, and community building.
